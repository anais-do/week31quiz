My favorite part of Advanced Web

My favorite part of Patino is the environment it provides. Both the interior and student climate is nice. For example, all the rooms are not over filled with people and provide a good space where groups of people can work. Also, the students here are much friendlier than those of other high schools.

My least favorite part of Advanced Web

My least favorite part about Patino is the school spirit. Since there are only about 300 students here, there is not a lot of people who partake in school activities. For example, our dances are quiet as well as our rallies.
